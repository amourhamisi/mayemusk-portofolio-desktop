Who is Maye?

In the past fifty years, Maye has started her nutrition business in eight cities and three countries through speaking, consulting, counseling, writing and media work. It was never easy to start over, but each move was worth the effort.

Maye also has a second career. She has been modeling since the age of 15, and this continued unexpectedly and consistently. Then, in her 60s, she had four billboards in Times Square and appeared on magazine covers such as Vogue, Marie Claire, Elle, Sports Illustrated and Harpers Bazaar. She now focuses on speaking engagements as her book, A Woman Makes A Plan, Advice for a Lifetime of Adventure, Beauty and Success, is a bestseller and translated into thirty languages. It is available in over one hundred countries.

She continues to give presentations on entrepreneurship, aging, gender equality, single parenting, overcoming challenges, confidence and health at conferences, events and universities, inspiring audiences worldwide.

Her work takes her to many cities where she learns about different cultures and shares her experiences. She speaks Afrikaans fluently, and functional Dutch, French and German, often doing interviews in these languages.

A Woman Makes a Plan

ADVICE FOR A LIFETIME OF ADVENTURE, BEAUTY, AND SUCCESS

In A Woman Makes a Plan, Maye shares experiences from her life conveying hard-earned wisdom on career (the harder you work, the luckier you get), family (let the people you love go their own way), health (there is no magic pill), and adventure (make room for discovery, but always be ready for anything). You can't control all that happens in life, but you can have the life you want at any age. All you have to do is make a plan.
